State And Cente (Staff Selection Commissions) conducts typing skill tests for different types of posts vacant in various government offices/departments. Commissions follows a specific criteria for conducting these typing tests. This software, made with an interface similar to the one used by HPSSC Hamirpur helps you to enhance your typing skills by practicing on this platform. Also, the WPM calculator helps you to calculate your net typing speed.
